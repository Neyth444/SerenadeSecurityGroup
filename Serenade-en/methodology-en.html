Our Cybersecurity Audit Methodology

Cybersecurity is no improvisation. At Serenade, we follow a rigorous and internationally recognized approach (OWASP, PTES) to ensure reliable and actionable audits. All our tests are conducted in a non-destructive and controlled manner, without impacting your services.

1. Initial contact & scoping

We start with a discussion to understand your business and technical challenges: type of activity, sensitive data, and the scope to be audited.

  • Clear definition of the scope (website, network, cloud, users)
  • Choice of audit type (internal, external, compliance, awareness)
  • Signing of an NDA to protect your information

2. Technical tests & analysis

We combine cutting-edge tools and human expertise to simulate what an attacker might attempt, but in a secure and controlled environment.

  • Realistic tests: SQL injections, authentication flaws, XSS
  • Network and server configuration analysis
  • Standards followed: OWASP Top 10, MITRE ATT&CK
  • Professional tools: Nmap, Burp Suite, WPScan…

3. Clear and prioritized report

You receive an educational and actionable report. No unnecessary jargon: a practical tool for both executives and technical teams.

  • Executive summary for decision-makers
  • Vulnerability table with severity levels
  • Visual evidence (screenshots, logs, demos)
  • Prioritized remediation plan

4. Follow-up & support

We do not apply fixes ourselves, but we assist your IT team or technical provider to ensure they are implemented effectively.

  • Technical support and guidance during remediation
  • Awareness workshops for your employees
  • Re-test available after fixes
  • Ongoing follow-up to maintain a high security level

A structured and transparent approach

With Serenade, you always know what we do, why we do it, and how to fix the issues we identify. Our methodology protects your data, reduces financial risks, and preserves your reputation.
