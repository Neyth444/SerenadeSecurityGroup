What is a Cybersecurity Audit?

A cybersecurity audit is a comprehensive review of your IT systems, networks, and security policies. Its goal: identify vulnerabilities and provide solutions to fix them. It prevents attacks before they occur, protects sensitive data, and preserves the trust of your clients and partners.
